💎
22% of customers drive 61% of total revenue
Premium Spenders pay in full, have low utilisation, and spend consistently. Retaining and rewarding this group should be the #1 priority.
✈️
Travel spend spikes 340% in summer
June–August travel transactions surge dramatically. Pre-summer campaigns in April/May capture peak demand before competitors act.
⚠️
High utilisation = high default risk
Customers above 75% credit utilisation are 4× more likely to make minimum-only payments — a key early warning indicator for risk teams.
🍔
Food & Dining is the single biggest category
28% of all transactions. Partnering with food delivery or restaurant platforms would meaningfully increase card engagement and spend.
Financial Product Recommendations
01
Premium Travel Rewards Card
For top 22% — cashback on Travel and Dining, the categories driving their highest spend.
02
Early Intervention — At-Risk Segment
Proactive outreach before balances worsen — hardship plan or balance transfer offer.
03
Summer Travel Campaign
Pre-season promotion in April targeting Growth Customers — capture the 340% summer spend surge.
04
Dormant Reactivation
Personalised cashback on last active spend category for the 26% dormant customers.
