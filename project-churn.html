📋
Contract type is the #1 churn driver
Month-to-month customers churn at 42% vs 11% annual — a 4× difference. Converting monthly to annual is the single highest-ROI retention action available.
⏱️
First 90 days are the highest-risk window
Customers with under 3 months tenure show the sharpest churn spike. Onboarding investment here has the highest return of any single intervention.
🛠️
Tech support doubles retention
Customers without tech support are 2.1× more likely to churn. Bundling basic support into entry plans is estimated to reduce churn by 18%.
💰
High charges + low tenure = danger zone
Customers paying €65+/month in their first 6 months have a 63% churn rate. Introductory pricing in this window reduces this by an estimated 28%.
Business Recommendations
01
90-Day Loyalty Programme
Automated check-in and discount at day 30, 60, and 90 for all new customers.
02
Bundle Tech Support
Include basic tech support in entry plans — reduces churn by a projected 18%.
03
Annual Contract Incentive
Offer 15% discount to convert month-to-month customers at the 3-month mark.
04
High-Risk Segment Alerts
Flag red-segment customers for proactive outreach before they reach cancellation.
