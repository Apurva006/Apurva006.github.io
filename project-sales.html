🌍
West Region dominates — replicate its playbook
West outperformed Central by 45% in revenue. Central's margin (7.8%) suggests pricing and discount issues that West has already solved.
📉
Discounts above 30% consistently destroy margin
Orders with 30%+ discount had negative average profit. 18% of all Central orders fell in this band — the primary driver of its underperformance.
🗓️
Q4 seasonality is predictable — plan ahead
Q4 generated 34.8% of annual revenue across all 4 years. Inventory and campaigns should be planned 8 weeks in advance consistently.
🏢
Corporate segment has highest order value
Corporate averages €280/order vs €196 for Consumer. B2B campaigns and volume bundles could grow this segment from 31% to 40%.
Business Recommendations
01
Cap Discounts at 20% in Central
Enforce a 20% ceiling — projected to recover €62K in lost annual margin.
02
Replicate West's Strategy
Audit West's pricing and account management; deploy in Central by Q2.
03
Q4 Campaign Planning
Start inventory build and marketing 8 weeks before Q4 to capture seasonal demand.
04
Grow Corporate Segment
Launch B2B loyalty programme — Corporate delivers 43% more revenue per order.
