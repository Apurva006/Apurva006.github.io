Three Specialties Account for Over 40% of Ireland's Longest Waits — and They've Never Recovered to Pre-Pandemic Levels

Orthopaedics, Dermatology, and Ophthalmology have consistently carried the highest backlog pressure across every year of this analysis. While other specialties showed partial post-COVID recovery, these three remain above pre-pandemic levels.

Waiting lists grew by an average of 18% between 2019 and 2023. The number of patients waiting over 18 months in Orthopaedics alone exceeds the total waiting list of several smaller specialties combined.

This analysis argues that targeted investment in three specialties would deliver more impact than blanket spending — with Orthopaedics accounting for 22% of all patients waiting more than 12 months nationally.

🔴
3 specialties carry 40%+ of Ireland's longest waits
Orthopaedics, Dermatology, and Ophthalmology appear in the top-pressure group every year 2019–2024. This is a structural problem, not a temporary backlog.
📈
No full recovery post-Covid for critical specialties
While some areas improved in 2024, the three critical specialties remain 18–40% above pre-pandemic baselines — evidence of structural under-capacity.
⚠️
Urology and ENT showing early warning signs
Both are rising before reaching crisis level. Without intervention, they are likely to join the critical tier within 2–3 years based on current trends.
💡
Targeted intervention beats blanket spending
Directing resources specifically at Orthopaedics and Dermatology would address 40%+ of the 18-month+ backlog at a fraction of system-wide expansion costs.
Policy Recommendations for HSE Leadership
01
NTPF Outsourcing — Dermatology
Routine procedures suitable for private capacity outsourcing. Estimated 30% wait reduction within 12 months.
02
Physio-Led Triage — Orthopaedics
Redirect 35–40% of referrals to physiotherapy-led assessment, reducing unnecessary consultant appointments.
03
Community Dermatology Clinics
GPWSI clinics in primary care — proven to reduce outpatient pressure by 25% in comparable NHS pilots.
04
Early Warning System — Urology & ENT
Monthly threshold alerts to trigger capacity planning before lists become chronic and irreversible.
